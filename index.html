NARUTO

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The series is set in a fictional land loosely based on Japanese feudal society, and Naruto overcomes challenges through hard work and perseverance.

NARUTO-SHIPPUDEN

Naruto Shippuden is an anime series that follows the story of Naruto Uzamaki, who was born on the day the village of Konoha was attacked by the 9-tailed fox demon. Naruto's father, the 4th hokage, sacrificed his life and sealed the demon in his newborn son. 13 years later, Naruto graduates from the ninja academy and becomes a shinobi with the goal of becoming the hokage of the village. In the series, Naruto leaves his village for intense exercises that will push him to his limits to become the best ninja in the land.

DEATH NOTE

Death Note is a Japanese mangathat follows Light Yagami, a genius high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook called the "Death Note". The notebook belonged to the shinigami Ryuk and grants the user the supernatural ability to kill anyone whose name is written in its pages. The story revolves around Light's use of the Death Note to rid the world of evil and the efforts of a secretive detective known as L to find him.

ONE PIECE

One Piece is a story about Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, who explore the Grand Line in search of the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates. The treasure was left behind by the former pirate king Gol D. Roger. The story is an epic saga of adventure on the high seas, following the adventures of Luffy, an enthusiastic young man with the singular aspiration of becoming King of the Pirates

MY HERO ACADEMIA

My Hero Academia is a manga and anime series set in a world where superpowers (called "Quirks") are commonplace. The story follows Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without a Quirk, who dreams of becoming a superhero. He meets the world's greatest hero, All Might, who shares his powers with Izuku and enrolls him in a prestigious high school for superheroes in training.

BLEACH

Bleach (stylized in all caps) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo. It follows the adventures of a teenager Ichigo Kurosaki, who obtains the powers of a Soul Reaper —a death personification similar to a Grim Reaper —from another Soul Reaper, Rukia Kuchiki.

SOLO LEVELING

Solo Leveling is a Webtoon series that's been making waves. Despite its seemingly generic premise and story, the series has surprisingly impressive world-building and art that make it perfect for any reader itching for a new action series to delve into. With its second season begun, now is an opportune time to catch up and delve right in. The story takes place in a world where mysterious portals known as Gates have sprung up around the globe, connecting the human world to alternate dimensions. Simultaneously, something has awakened within humans, granting them supernatural abilities to fight the monsters found in these Gates. These humans came to be known as Hunters and are ranked by letter grade from E to S. With the establishment of Hunter agencies and guilds, it became a career for individuals to enter Gates and slay the bosses that keep them open for profit.

JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen is a supernatural action anime that follows high school student Yuuji Itadori as he battles against magical curses. After Yuji selflessly saves a classmate by taking a curse into his own body, he is discovered and trained by a powerful sorcerer named Satoru Gojo. The series is an action-packed dark fantasy that revolves around Yuji's journey to eliminate a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna, of whom Yuji becomes the host.

DEMON SLAYER

Demon Slayer is a manga and anime series that follows Tanjiro Kamado, a teenage boy who joins the Demon Slayer Corps to turn his sister Nezuko back into a human. The series is set in the Taishō era Japan, where demons are former humans who possess supernatural abilities and prey on humans. Tanjiro and his allies face various challenges and enemies as they fight to protect humanity and find a cure for Nezuko.

VINLAND SAGA

TOKYO REVENGERS

Tokyo Revengers is an anime series that follows the story of Hanagaki Takemichi, a 26-year-old boy who travels back in time to his middle school years in 2005 after being pushed in front of a train. He does this to prevent the death of his middle school ex-girlfriend Hinata Tachibana and her younger brother Naoto, who were killed by the Tokyo Manji Gang. In order to save them, he must become the leader of a dreaded school gang.

BLACK CLOVER

Black Clover is a manga and anime series set in a world where people are born with magic abilities. The story follows Asta, a young boy without magic power, who receives a rare grimoire that grants him anti-magic abilities. Along with his fellow mages from the Black Bulls, Asta aims to become the next Wizard King.

CHAINSAW MAN

Chainsaw Man follows the story of Denji, an impoverished teenager who makes a contract that fuses his body with that of a dog-like Devil named Pochita, granting him the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws.

KAIJU NO8

Kaiju No. 8 is a manga series about Kafka Hibino, a man who gains the power to transform into a kaiju. He wants to join the Japan Defense Force, a military organization that fights kaiju, to fulfill a promise he made with a childhood friend. The series is licensed by Viz Media and has an anime adaptation in the works by Production I.G. and Studio Khara.

SPY X FAMILY

Created by Tatsuya Endo, Spy X Family is an action-adventure comedy anime that follows the misadventures of a found family with unique quirks. The story follows spy Twilight (known as Loid Forger), who, while on assignment, builds a fake family to get closer to his target.

DAN DA DAN

Dan Da Dan, a romcom anime by Science Saru, blends high school romance with paranormal antics, eagerly anticipated this year. Science Saru projects prioritize uniqueness, embracing a variety of visual styles, creating open-minded, project-driven anime productions.
