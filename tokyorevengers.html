Mikey

Manjiro Sano, otherwise called "Mikey," is the leader of the Tokyo Manji Gang and the most beloved Tokyo Revengers character on MyAnimeList. He's a merciless commander who will do anything possible to lead the gang to victory. Beneath Mikey's lighthearted exterior, however, he's dealing with immense mental distress. Falling prey to his dark impulses, Mikey becomes one of the most compelling characters to analyze in the entire series

Chifuyu Matsuno

Chifuyu is the first division vice-captain of the Tokyo Manji Gang. Praised for his intelligence and fierce loyalty, Chifuyu quickly became a fan favorite after his anime debut. In middle school, Chifuyu was staunchly against authority. He would take every opportunity to blatantly disrespect his superiors, especially those who abused their power.

Draken

Ken Ryuguji, commonly referred to as "Draken," instantly stole fans' hearts. As one of Tokyo Manji Gang's founding members and vice-president, Draken is a wildcard like many of his comrades. Always lusting after the next big fight, Draken has never been one to back down from a challenge.

Keisuke Baji

Baji was one of the founding members of the Tokyo Manji Gang. He's remembered as a daredevil and fiercely loyal comrade. To protect the gang, he pretended to join Valhalla, where he essentially acts as a double agent. His willingness to participate in such a perilous venture is nothing less than admirable.

Takemichi Hanagaki

Despite being the protagonist, Takemichi isn't the most popular character in the series. His arc is relatively polarizing, and a common complaint is that his extreme emotions derail the entire show. That said, Takemichi's emotions are his greatest strength — without them, he wouldn't be recognized as the "Crybaby Hero."

Takashi Mitsuya

Takashi is one of the Tokyo Manji Gang's founding members. Fans appreciate Takashi for his level-headed personality and adorable relationship with his younger siblings, Luna and Mana. Currently, Takashi has a career in fashion design, and boasts 1,948 favorites on MyAnimeList.

Kazutora Hanemiya

Kazutora quickly became a beloved character after his debut in the anime. His spunky, outgoing demeanor hides much of his inner turmoil — and that's precisely what makes him so interesting. Kazutora was a founding member of the Tokyo Manji Gang, but he betrayed them after mistakenly murdering Mikey's brother.

Hinata Tachibana

Hinata Tachibana's death is the inciting incident for the entire series. If she hadn't been killed, Takemichi wouldn't have had to time travel, and fans wouldn't have gotten such a fantastic story. Hinata has 584 favorite votes on MyAnimeList.

Shuji Hanma

Shuji has been in several gangs throughout Tokyo Revengers. Before joining the Kanto Manji Gang, he used to be one of Tetta Kisaki's closest allies. Acting as Tetta's confidant, he would orchestrate several attacks on the Tokyo Manji Gang, ultimately seeking to overthrow their current leadership.

Nahoya Kawata

Nahoya Kawata, more commonly referred to as "Smiley," earned 333 favorites on MyAnimeList. Although he hasn't had much presence in the anime yet, Nahoya is sure to win the hearts of anime-only fans, just as he did with avid manga readers.
