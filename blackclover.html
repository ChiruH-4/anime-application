Lucius Zogratis

Lucius Zogratis, the eldest Zogratis sibling, is recognized as the host of the devil Astaroth. With a distinctive sun-like mark on his forehead, he stands out among his peers. While harboring two souls, the other being Julius Novachrono, he reveals his potential as an unmatched devil host. Lucius, driven by a belief in human supremacy and a messiah complex, aims to reshape humanity using the devil’s power. Engaging with formidable foes, including Lucifero, his ambitions, and magic abilities pose significant challenges for the Clover Kingdom and its defenders.

Lucifero

Lucifero is a high-ranking devil in the Black Clover universe. Sinister and powerful, he seeks to manifest in the human world fully. As the devil connected to the Dark Triad, especially Dante, Lucifero’s influence causes a series of conflicts and battles within the series, posing significant challenges to the heroes of the story.

Asta

Asta is the main protagonist of Black Clover. Born without magic in a world where magic is everything, he compensates with sheer determination and physical training. RELATED:Asta obtains a five-leaf grimoire with anti-magic capabilities, setting him on a path to achieve his dream of becoming the Wizard King. His unwavering spirit, coupled with his strong sense of justice, makes him a beacon of hope for many.

Licht

Licht was the leader of the Elf Tribe and wielded a four-leaf grimoire with light magic. Known for his calm demeanor and desire for peace, Licht’s life took a tragic turn when humans betrayed the elves, leading to a massacre. His actions and the aftermath of this betrayal have long-lasting consequences in the Black Clover universe, influencing several events in the story

Yuno Grinberryal

Yuno Grinberryall is Asta’s foster brother and lifelong rival. Found alongside Asta outside the Hage Village church, Yuno is a prodigious magic user, wielding wind magic and a four-leaf grimoire. His calm and stoic demeanor contrasts with Asta’s loud and energetic nature. Despite their differences, the two share a deep bond, pushing each other to achieve greater heights.

Morgen

Morgen Faust is Yami’s close friend and a fellow magic knight. While not extensively covered in the series, Morgen’s influence on Yami and his principles are evident. Unlike his dark magic-wielding friend, Morgen was known to be gentle and kind, making their bond unique and highlighting the complementary nature of their relationship

Yami Sukehiro

Yami Sukehiro, captain of the Black Bulls, is a foreigner hailing from the Land of the Sun. Wielding dark magic and a katana, Yami’s rough-around-the-edges demeanor hides a deep sense of camaraderie. He often pushes his squad members to surpass their limits, believing in their potential even when others don’t. His laid-back attitude, combined with his sharp insights, makes him a unique and beloved character in the series.

Lily Aquaria

Lily Aquaria is a nun at the Hage Village church in the Black Clover universe. She is known to be kind-hearted caring for the orphans of the village, including Asta and Yuno. Though not a central figure in the series’ overarching narrative, her influence on Asta’s and Yuno’s upbringing is significant, providing them with love, care, and moral guidance.

Acier Silv

Acier Silva, mother to Nozel and his siblings, was a renowned magic knight known for her steel magic. As the former captain of the Silver Eagle squad, she was revered for her strength and grace. A loving mother, Acier’s death under mysterious circumstances had a profound impact on her children, especially Nozel, who harbored guilt and pain related to her passing.

Fuegoleon Vermillion

Fuegoleon Vermillion is the captain of the Crimson Lion squad and a respected figure in the Clover Kingdom. Like his sister Mereoleona, he wields fire magic but with a more strategic and calm demeanor. A symbol of justice and nobility, Fuegoleon inspires loyalty and admiration from those around him. His leadership qualities and sense of duty make him a pillar of strength for the Clover Kingdom’s defense.
